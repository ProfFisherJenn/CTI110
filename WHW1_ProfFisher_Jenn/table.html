Chromatic Clearance Levels

AlgoCratic Futures™ operates through a structured chromatic clearance system. Each level represents an ascending stage of authorized awareness — what you are permitted to know, what you are permitted to feel about what you know, and what you are permitted to suspect you do not know. Clearance is not earned. Clearance is determined. Your current level has already been assigned. This page is provided as a courtesy.
